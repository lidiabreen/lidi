Welcome to Lidia Breen's GitHub Page.

My name is Lidia Breen. I am currently a sophomore at Lehigh University, double-majoring in Politicial Science and Journalism. This website is a portfoilo of data vizualizations I have created in a seven-week Data Vizualization course at Lehigh. Check out some of the links below to see infographics, timelines, graphs, maps and other cool things!

First Projects

The first projects we took on in Data Vizualization were building a simple infographic and a simple chart. For the infographic, we worked on Canva to create a .png file that we could then post to the internet. For the simple chart, we used ChartBuilder, for which we would input the data we selected, and then the program would create a basline chart that we could edit from that point. Here is the link to view my infographic: lidiabreen.github.io/growingpolitics.html and here is the link to view my chart: lidiabreen.github.io/LUEnrollment2015.html

Timelines and Maps

We also worked with supplemental programs on the Gmail Google Drive, including Google Fusion Charts and a timeline program. This allowed me to create an interactive google map that showed different resteraunts in the Bethlehem, PA area. Here that one is: lidiabreen.github.io/Breakfast.html . We also created a timeline of something that interested us. Since I am on the soccer team at Lehigh, I decided to look up a brief history of the Lehigh Women's Soccer team, and create a timeline out of it. Here it is: lidiabreen.github.io/LUWS.html
